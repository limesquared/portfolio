Hello, I'm Elizabeth!

About Me

I was born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania on November 21st, 1999. My mom is from Thailand while my dad is from Cambodia, although I can't speak either Thai/Laos or Khmer, I do mostly understand it. I currently attend High Point Central as a rising senior and plan on applying to UNC Greensboro, Appalachian State, and UNC Chapel Hill as my top three choices. My current choice of major is psychology. I have experience in graphic design programs such as Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator, as well as 3D Studio Max and GameMaker. Apparently, I have a knack for design.

Likes

In my spare time, I enjoy reading novels, writing fiction, and playing video games. Occasionally I draw, but not often. My favorite genre of fiction to read and write is fantasy, while my favorite authors include Ed Greenwood, R.A. Salvatore, and Stephen King. I also like sweet things and playing chess. A lot of my time is wasted on websites like TVTropes, Reddit, and window-shopping on Amazon.

Dislikes

I'm a very introverted person so I hate giving ad lib presentations, or really just presentations in front of crowds in general. I hate extremely crowded spaces and elevators, and hot humid weather. Spiders freak me out, I once found a two-inch long spider crawling up my leg, making me lose at least five years of my life. Also, people who breathe extremely loudly make me uncomfortable.
